Korean Cuisine

Side Dish

Kimchi

Fermented vegetables, mainly cabbage, with spicy seasoning, a Korean staple.

Ingredients

  • Napa Cabbage
  • Red Chili Pepper
  • Garlic & Fish Sauce

Method: Salt cabbage, mix with seasoning, ferment for several days.

Main

Bibimbap

Mixed rice with vegetables, meat, egg, and spicy gochujang sauce.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Vegetables & Meat
  • Egg & Gochujang

Method: Arrange ingredients over rice, mix thoroughly before eating.

Snack

Tteokbokki

Chewy rice cakes cooked in sweet-spicy gochujang sauce.

Ingredients

  • Rice Cakes
  • Gochujang
  • Fish Cake & Vegetables

Method: Simmer ingredients in sauce until rice cakes are soft and coated.
