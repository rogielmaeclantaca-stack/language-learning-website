Filipino Cuisine

Main

Adobo

Classic Filipino stew made with meat, vinegar, soy sauce, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • Pork or Chicken
  • Soy sauce & Vinegar
  • Garlic & Bay leaves

Method: Marinate meat, saut√©, simmer with sauce until tender.

Main

Sinigang

Sour soup with pork, shrimp, or fish, and local vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Pork, Shrimp or Fish
  • Tamarind base
  • Vegetables

Method: Boil meat/fish, add vegetables and sour base, simmer until cooked.

Main

Lechon

Crispy roasted pig, a festive Filipino favorite.

Ingredients

  • Whole pig
  • Salt, spices
  • Lemongrass & marinade

Method: Stuff pig, season, roast slowly until skin is crispy.
