Italian Cuisine

Main

Pizza Margherita

Classic Neapolitan pizza with tomato, mozzarella, and basil.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella & Basil

Method: Spread sauce on dough, add toppings, bake at high heat 10-15 min.

Main

Pasta Carbonara

Rich pasta with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs & Pecorino
  • Pancetta & Pepper

Method: Cook pasta, combine with eggs, cheese, and cooked pancetta off heat.

Dessert

Tiramisu

Coffee-flavored Italian dessert with mascarpone and cocoa.

Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers
  • Mascarpone & Eggs
  • Coffee & Cocoa

Method: Layer soaked ladyfingers with mascarpone mixture, chill 4h, dust cocoa.
